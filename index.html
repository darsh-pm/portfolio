How I work

Strategy that survives contact with engineering.

◎

Product discovery

Start from the user's actual problem, not the requested feature. Requirements gathering across 30+ client projects taught me to find the question behind the ask.

Ruthless prioritization

Every backlog is a negotiation between impact and cost. I make the tradeoffs explicit, sequence by leverage, and say no with reasons attached.
